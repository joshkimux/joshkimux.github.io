  • I'm a flexible designer with consulting chops.

    I've led discovery teams, facilitated participatory design workshops, prototyped high-fidelity flows, tested for WCAG 2.1 compliance, and presented at major client meetings.

    For a laundry list of my skills and tools, check out my resume.

  • My past experience can speak for itself.

    Don't take my word for it: check out my portfolio. I've worked with large government agencies, state healthcare, the US Digital Response, SNHU, Amtrak, and more.
