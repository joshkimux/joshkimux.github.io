Design Philosophy

I found my purpose and direction in design through the words of my heroes.

  • "Exclusion and inclusion are a series of choices designers make every day. Period."

    Kat Holmes

  • "Quit imagining you’re brilliant enough to automate something as human—as rare—as delight, and go fix your floorboards."

    Sara Wachter-Boettcher

  • "Be present for the whole journey, learners that you are. Be present for the whole journey."

    Halcolm (Michael Patton)

  • "Stop blaming everybody else for the problem or pretending that it is too hard for us to solve. Let’s put that spirit of innovation and embrace of radical change to good use."

    Emily Chang

  • "We need to fear the consequences of our work more than we love the cleverness of our ideas."

    Mike Monteiro

Josh, the Human Being

Beyond my working hours, I find my happiness by giving back to those around me.

From cultivating local culture through art, making AIGA DC's first online design week accessible, to teaching design thinking to kids- I try to be a helper when I can.

When I'm not volunteering or studying, you can find me on the tennis courts, writing music, or abroad with friends at the Shamrock Pub in Hongdae.

This isn't a generic squarespace portfolio. It's a living work-in-progress that I redesign annually to better represent my growing values and skills as a designer. That being said, it still needs alot of work.

Read more about my commitment to improve accessibility or see what changes I'm making to my portfolio right now on trello.
