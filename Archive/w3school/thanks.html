What's Next?

Congrats, we just finished building a mobile webapp- or well, at least the front end of it. From this point on you'll need to give your design over to the back-end code monkeys to solve. Alot of the tasks necessary to hook up your code will require communication with your developers, and you may find yourself coming back (99% chance you will) to make edits to add div IDs, change layouts, etc.

If you ever get lost moving forwards, be sure to check out this full hands on tutorial of W3.CSS from the people who made it.

You can also download the demo WINER website I built here if you need extra reference points!
