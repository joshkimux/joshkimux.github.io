Restore Dignity

It's hard to be a survivor, but it's easy to support one.

We are dedicated to supporting the education of Survivors of campus sexual assault.

Restore Dignity is a non-profit, charitable organization that provides financial assistance to Survivors of power-inequality trauma, including sexual assault, intimate partner violence, domestic violence, stalking, or hazing during their pursuit of higher education.

Our mission is to engage, unite, and empower Survivors by reducing the financial burdens accrued in the aftermath of an unjust trauma.

We're giving equal access to education for Survivors through direct financial assistance.

Tuition

Do you need to reduce your course load and take an extra semester? We can help pay for an extra course.

Housing

Do you need to move to a safer environment? We can help cover rent.

Counseling

Do you need assistance in paying for counseling services? We can help pay these medical bills.

Lifestyle

Do you need to leave your part-time job or need help paying for your meal plan? We can subsidize these lifestyle changes.
